About Us

Sihiri City was founded in 2021 during a space launch to the moon; although the space rocket never made it to its planned destination a habitable meteor large enough to live on was discovered. Sihiri City sits about 3,700 miles from Earth and rotates counter clockwise around the planet. What makes Sihiri so attractive to new comers is the protective dome that surround the city; no one that lives in Sihiri can die, as long as they remain inside the dome.
