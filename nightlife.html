Nightlife

Nightlife in Shihiri is an expereince that cannot be imitiated, duplicated or reciprocated anywhere else!!! A visit to Shihiri Mile will leave you breathless and in awe. The blue lit dome that covers this party epicenter is soundproof in order to cut down on the distraction during the workday. With our bars and clubs open 24/7, 365 you can always escape the bustle of the big city and step away from the reality of your everyday life. With state of the art fixtures and lighting, world renowned DJ's on the turntables and the best live performers you'll ever see, you'll never want to leave. Come experience all that we have to offer!
